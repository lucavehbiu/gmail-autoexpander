Frequently Asked Questions

Is this extension safe?

Absolutely! Gmail Unlimited is open-source, processes everything locally in your browser, and collects zero data. You can review the source code on GitHub.

Does it work with Gmail business accounts?

Yes! Gmail Unlimited works with both personal Gmail (@gmail.com) and Google Workspace accounts.

Will this slow down Gmail?

No. The extension includes smart rate limiting (max 5 expansions per second) and efficient detection to ensure Gmail stays fast and responsive.

Is it really free?

Yes, completely free and open-source under the MIT license. No premium features, no ads, no subscriptions.

Can I turn it off temporarily?

Yes! You can disable auto-expansion in the extension settings and use the manual expand button when needed.
